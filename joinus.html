JOIN US

IMPACT Lab is recruiting grad students to start fall 2024! Come Join us! You can learn more about the lab through our lab policy here!

Postdocs: We are looking for postdocs who share our passions for advancing a generalizable understanding of social cognition. Researchers interested in joining the IMPACT Lab are encouraged to email the PI. Please include a 1-page cover letter summarizing your past research experiences and potential research projects that you would like to pursue at the Lab, your CV, and one first-authored writing sample (e.g., published journal article).

Grad Students: We are admitting graduate students during the 2023-2024 admission cycle. Students with backgrounds in psychology, social science, computer science, data science, and related fields who share our passions for IMPACT Lab's research directions are encouraged to apply. Learn more about UCSD Psychology Ph.D. program here and the admission process here.

Visiting Scholars: We welcome visiting grad students and postdocs around the world. Researchers interested in visiting the IMPACT Lab are encouraged to contact the PI to discuss potential projects. Please include your CV and research interests. You could learn more about UCSD visitor policies here.

Lab Managers: The lab is not currently looking for lab managers. Please check back with this page in the future if you are interested in joining the lab as a lab manager.

Research Assistants: The lab is currently looking for undergraduate research assistants to involve in an ongoing project. Please research out to the PI if you are interested to learn more about the project, how you could get involved, and what you can expect to learn.
